Overview

Project Overview

A comprehensive Invoice Management System built with Laravel 11, designed for real business operations. The system goes beyond basic CRUD — it's structured around a permission-driven RBAC model, normalized relational schema, and clearly defined invoice state transitions to ensure data integrity at every step.

The modular design supports horizontal expansion, making it multi-company ready. Primary emphasis was on database modeling, separation of concerns, and a maintainable service structure — ensuring the system remains extensible under increasing business complexity.

Features

Invoice Management
  • Smart Dashboard — View invoice stats and payment percentages (Paid, Unpaid, Partial) using interactive charts
  • Full CRUD — Add, edit, delete, archive, and print professional invoices
  • Payment Status Tracking — Update invoice status (Paid / Partially Paid / Unpaid) with payment date recording
  • Attachment Management — Upload, download, preview, and delete invoice-related attachments (Images, PDFs, etc.)
  • Notifications — Built-in notification system triggered on new invoice activities
Reporting & Export
  • Invoices Report — Search and filter by invoice number, type, or date range
  • Customers Report — Search by section, product, and date range with aggregated totals
  • Excel Export — Export invoice lists and full reports directly to Excel via Maatwebsite

Architecture

Design Decisions
RBAC with Spatie — Permission-driven access model. Every action is gated by granular permissions, not just role checks.
Normalized Schema — Relational design ensures data integrity with proper foreign keys, constraints, and no redundant data.
State Machine — Invoice transitions (Draft → Issued → Paid → Archived) are validated server-side, preventing illegal state jumps.
Multi-company Ready — Modular design supports horizontal expansion. Adding a new company context requires no structural changes.
Separation of Concerns — Service layer handles business logic independently of controllers, keeping the codebase clean and testable.
